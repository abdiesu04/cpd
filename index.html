📜 Match Rules & Process

📍 Round 1: 16 participants compete to secure the Top 8 quarterfinalists.
📍 Quarterfinals: Top 8 compete to determine the Top 4 semifinalists.
📍 Semifinals: Top 4 battle to reach the Top 2 Finalists.
🏆 Finals: The Top 2 finalists compete for the championship, while semifinal losers face off to secure Third Place 🥉.

🕔 Each round lasts 10 minutes.
❔ One problem is given in each round.
🥇 The first solver from each pair advances to the next stage.
